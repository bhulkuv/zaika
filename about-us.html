Our Mission

At Zaika, we’re driven by the belief that food brings people together. We’re here to share not just the flavors of India but also the stories and care behind every dish. When you dine with us, you’re not just having a meal—you’re part of a journey that blends tradition, creativity, and community.

Our Story

Zaika started with a simple idea: to share the flavors and traditions of Indian cooking in a way that feels both familiar and exciting. Growing up, food was more than just a meal—it was a way to connect with family, celebrate, and explore creativity. When we opened Zaika at 1100 Pike St, Seattle, WA, we wanted to bring that same feeling to our guests, blending the rich traditions of Indian cuisine with local ingredients and modern presentation.

What Zaika Means to Us

In Hindi, “Zaika” means “taste,” but for us, it’s much more. It’s about creating moments of surprise and joy when something truly special is experienced. Every dish we serve is crafted with care, blending traditional Indian recipes with the best of what Washington has to offer. From the spices in our Kashmiri Malai Kofta to the local touches in our Jackfruit Kofta, we aim to offer something unique and unforgettable.

Indian Cuisine with a Modern Twist

At Zaika, we respect tradition but aren’t afraid to try new things. Our Chicken Tikka Masala might be a classic, but it’s plated in a way that feels fresh and modern. By using local ingredients and creative techniques, we’ve found a way to make Indian food approachable for everyone while still honoring its roots. Whether you’re new to Indian cuisine or a lifelong fan, there’s something here for you.
