Welcome to Zaika

A Taste of India in Seattle

At Zaika, located at 1100 Pike St, Seattle, WA, we bring the rich flavors of Indian cuisine to the heart of the city. Our name means “taste,” but for us, it’s about so much more. It’s about creating moments of joy and discovery every time you enjoy our food. Whether you’re joining us for a casual meal, a family gathering, or a night out with friends, we’re here to make every visit special.

Fresh, Authentic, and Made Daily

Every dish at Zaika is prepared fresh each day, using the finest ingredients to bring traditional Indian recipes to life. From the creamy, flavorful Chicken Tikka Masala to the unique Jackfruit Kofta and the rich Kashmiri Malai Kofta, our menu offers something for everyone. We’ve carefully designed our dishes to combine the best of Indian cooking with a modern touch, making our food approachable and exciting for all.
